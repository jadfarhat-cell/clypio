Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This policy explains what information Clypio collects, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. It applies to our website, our client and clipper dashboards, and everyone who applies to work with us.

1. Who we are

Clypio is a performance clipping network. Brands fund campaigns and pay for verified reach; independent creators ("clippers") make and post short-form clips and get paid for the views those clips genuinely earn. Clypio is operated by [Clypio legal entity, confirm before publishing] ("Clypio", "we", "us"). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@clypio.com.

2. What we collect

We only collect what we need to run the network. What we hold depends on how you interact with us.

If you're a brand or client

  • Your name, email, company, and what you want to promote.
  • Campaign details and budgets you set.
  • Payment information you enter to fund a campaign. Card details are handled by our payment processor, Stripe, and are never stored on Clypio's servers.

If you're a clipper

  • Your name, email, social handles, and the platforms you post on.
  • The clips you submit (post links) and the public performance of those posts.
  • Payout details you provide so we can pay you.

If you apply to work with us

  • Everything in the application form, including any sample clip you send us for review.

Everyone who visits

  • Basic technical data your browser sends (such as IP address and device or browser type), used to keep the service secure and working.
  • Strictly necessary cookies or tokens that keep you signed in. We do not sell your data or use it for third-party advertising.

3. How we use it

  • To operate the network: create accounts, run campaigns, and match clips to campaigns.
  • To verify views and detect fraud, including checking that a submitted clip was actually posted by the clipper who submitted it.
  • To process client payments and pay clippers.
  • To communicate with you: confirmations, account details, service updates, and support.
  • To meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

4. Who we share it with

We share information only with the service providers who help us run Clypio, and only as needed for them to do their job. These currently include:

  • Stripe: payment processing and payouts.
  • Resend: sending transactional email (confirmations, account details).
  • Google / YouTube Data API: reading the public view counts of submitted clips for verification.
  • Render and Vercel: hosting our application and website.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Clypio, our users, or others. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Public platform data

Our whole model depends on measuring real views. When a clipper submits a post, we read its publicly available data (such as view counts and the account that posted it) from the relevant platform to verify performance and guard against fraud. We only look at posts submitted to Clypio, and only at information the platform already makes public.

6. How long we keep it

We keep account, campaign, and payment records for as long as your account is active and for as long afterward as we need to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When we no longer need information, we delete or anonymize it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to request a copy of your data. To exercise any of these, email privacy@clypio.com and we'll respond within the timeframe the law requires. We won't discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

8. Security

We protect your information with encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls, and payment handling delegated to Stripe. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe and to respond quickly if something goes wrong.

9. International transfers

Clypio works with providers and users in different countries, and your information may be processed in a country other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for these transfers.

10. Age requirement

Clypio is not intended for anyone under 18. Clippers must be adults able to enter a contract and receive payment. We do not knowingly collect information from children, and we'll delete it if we learn we have.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Clypio evolves. When we make a material change, we'll update the date at the top and, where appropriate, let you know directly. Continuing to use Clypio after a change means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@clypio.com. For anything else, see our Terms of Service.
