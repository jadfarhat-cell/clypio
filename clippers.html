Our cut

Here's exactly how we make money.

Every campaign shows your rate per 1,000 verified views before you take it. Whatever the brand pays above that rate is our cut. We do not hide it, and we do not change it after you have already clipped. Here is what our share pays for.

Verification that protects your money
Every view gets checked against real platform data before anyone is paid. Bot farms get voided, so the pool goes to clippers who actually earned it, including you.
Guaranteed payout
Once your views are verified, you get paid, weekly. If a brand drags their feet on an invoice, that's our problem to chase, never yours.
Deal flow you never chase
We sign the brands, get the pools funded upfront, and handle the contracts. You never cold-DM a marketing manager. You clip and post.

No hidden fees, no surprise deductions, no fine print math. The rate on your dashboard is the rate that hits your account.
